Quizzer Help


Quizzer is a tool for
making and playing
quizzes. To begin, go
to the quiz builder.
To add a question, click
"add question". You will
notice that a question has
been added. To edit a
question, click on it.
When filling in the necessary
information remember that
when filling in the "correct answer"
box, fill it in with the correct
answer number, and not the correct
answer itself.
Still need help? Consider joining the Quizzer support server.
